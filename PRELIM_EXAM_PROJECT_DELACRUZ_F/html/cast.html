CHAINSAWMAN | CAST/STAFF

|AUTHOR|

Tatsuki Fujimoto
Tatsuki Fujimoto (Japanese: 藤本 タツキ, Hepburn: Fujimoto Tatsuki, born October 10, 1992 or 1993) is a Japanese manga artist, known for his works Fire Punch and Chainsaw Man.

|DIRECTED BY|
Ryu Nakayama
Ryu Nakayama helped MAPPA and its team live up to their aspirations and fans' high expectations for "Chainsaw Man." From start to finish.

|SCREENPLAY|
Koji/Hiroshi Seko
Hiroshi Seko is a Japanese anime screenwriter. After serving as an episode screenwriter, he was put in charge of all the screenwriting for Seraph of the End.

|CHARACTER DESIGN|
Kazutaka Sugiyama
Known for being the character designer & chief animation director of Chainsaw Man PV.

|ACTION DIRECTOR|
Tatsuya Yoshihara
Tatsuya Yoshihara is the action director for Chainsaw Man and recently has become one of the most influential figures in Japanese TV.

|CHIEF DIRECTOR|
Makoto Nakazono
Makoto Nakazono is known for Boogiepop and Others (2019) and A3! Season Spring & Summer (2020).

|DEVIL DESIGN|
Kiyotaka Oshiyama
Kiyotaka Oshiyama is known for Devilman: Crybaby (2018), The Wind Rises (2013) and The Secret World of Arrietty (2010).

|ART DIRECTOR|
Yuusuke Takeda
Takeda Yuusuke is an art director who was born on October 18, 1968 in Kamakura City, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan.

|COLOR DESIGN|
Naomi Nakano
Naomi NAKANO is best known for being the color design of REVENGER, Akiba Maid War, The Aquatope on White Sand, Chainsaw Man, and Sword Art Online

|PHOTOGRAPHY SUPERVISION|
Youhei Miyahara
Yohei Miyahara (宮原 洋平, Miyahara Yōhei) is a Japanese CG director and director of photography. He is the CEO of Capsule.

|MUSIC|
Kensuke Ushio
Kensuke Ushio is a Japanese composer, rock and EBM musician who performs under the moniker Agraph. He is a member of Japanese rock band Lama.

|ANIMAION PRODUCER|
Keisuke Seshita
Keisuke Seshimo's anime & manga roles. An animation producer affiliated with [MAPPA].

|PRODUCTION|
MAPPA
Japanese animation studio MAPPA (Maruyama Animation Produce Project Association) is widely known for its current streak of critically acclaimed productions, such as Attack on Titan Final Season, takt op.Destiny, and Jujutsu Kaisen.
